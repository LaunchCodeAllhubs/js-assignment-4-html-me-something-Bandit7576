Before

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to fly a jet? Well, riding a sportbike is the next best thing! Modern sportbikes are capable of speeds well in excess of 150 mph, the sensation of speed is increased exponentially though, thanks to the fact that you are on the machine and not inside it. Even at road legal speeds bikes will always feel faster, sportbikes in particular force you into a natural tuck position which also just feels fast, too.

Sportbikes are designed with speed in mind, every part of their design is there to help the rider operate effectively at speed. On track, they are most at home chasing the clock. Fun can be had on a canyon road too, the agility of the chassis can be taken to its limit and the acceleration out of a corner will make anyone feel like a racer.
